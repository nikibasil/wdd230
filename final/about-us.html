About Bountiful Foods

Bountiful Foods is a small owner owner-operated business specializing in fresh, organic, locally sourced foods. We work with local farmers to bring you the highest quality sustainable produce. We are located in Santa Barbara, California and have food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego. Our company sells fruits and produce directly from local farmers, as well as from national and international suppliers. We cater to walk-in traffic at our storefront in Santa Barbara and serve customers through our food trucks. We offer delivery services and accept orders the phone and via our website.

Our mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made to order, fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the local population and to tourists visiting the coastal areas. We pride ourselves in quality, covenience and superior sutomer service. We are here help support healthy eating, organic foods, sustainability.
